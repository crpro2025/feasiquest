Regulatory Requirements & Guidelines

Stay compliant with evolving DEI regulations

Key Regulatory Guidance

FDA Diversity Action Plans (2022)

Sponsors must submit diversity action plans for Phase 3 trials, including:

  • Current enrollment demographics
  • Enrollment goals by race, ethnicity, sex, age
  • Rationale for goals
  • Strategies to achieve goals

ICH E17: Multi-Regional Clinical Trials (2017)

Guidance on planning trials to support global regulatory submissions:

  • Consider ethnic factors in drug development
  • Include diverse populations early
  • Plan for regional differences
  • Ensure adequate representation

EMA Reflection Paper on Extrapolation (2018)

Emphasizes need for diverse data to support extrapolation:

  • Include relevant subpopulations
  • Consider genetic diversity
  • Account for environmental factors
  • Ensure broad applicability
