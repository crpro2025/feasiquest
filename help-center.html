‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with FeasiQuest?

+

Getting started is easy! First, create an account by clicking "Sign Up" and selecting your user type (Research Site, Sponsor, or CRO). Complete your profile with relevant information, and you'll be ready to start using the platform. For sponsors, you can immediately create studies and questionnaires. For sites, complete your profile to start receiving study opportunities.

What are the different user types?

+

FeasiQuest supports three user types:

  • Research Sites: Clinical research facilities that conduct trials
  • Sponsors: Pharmaceutical companies or organizations funding studies
  • CROs: Contract Research Organizations managing trials for sponsors

How does the questionnaire builder work?

+

The questionnaire builder offers 20+ question types including text, multiple choice, ratings, file uploads, and more. You can drag-and-drop questions, set up conditional logic, add scoring, and use pre-built templates. Questions can be organized into sections, and you can preview the questionnaire before publishing.

Can I edit my site profile after registration?

+

Yes! You can edit your profile anytime by going to your dashboard and clicking "Edit Profile" or navigating to the profile editor. You can update all information including capabilities, team members, photos, and documents.

How does AI site matching work?

+

Our AI analyzes your study requirements and matches them against site capabilities, experience, location, and performance metrics. The algorithm considers therapeutic area expertise, patient population, equipment availability, and historical performance to provide scored recommendations.

Is my data secure?

+

Yes, we take security seriously. FeasiQuest uses industry-standard encryption, secure data storage, and complies with HIPAA, GDPR, and other relevant regulations. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share your data without explicit permission.

What file formats can I upload?

+

We support common file formats including PDF, Word (DOC/DOCX), Excel (XLS/XLSX), PowerPoint (PPT/PPTX), images (JPG, PNG, GIF), and videos (MP4, MOV). Maximum file size is 25MB per file.

How do I contact support?

+

You can reach our support team via:

  • Email: support@feasiquest.com
  • Phone: 1-800-FEASIQUEST
  • Live chat (available 9am-5pm EST)
  • Contact form below

We typically respond within 24 hours.

Can I export my data?

+

Yes! You can export studies, questionnaires, responses, and reports in various formats including PDF, Excel, and CSV. Look for the export button in each section.

What are the pricing plans?

+

We offer flexible pricing for different user types:

  • Sites: Free to $799/month based on features
  • Sponsors: $499 to $4,999/month based on studies
  • CROs: Custom enterprise pricing

Visit our pricing page for detailed information.
